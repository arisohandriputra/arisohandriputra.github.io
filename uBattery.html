uBattery

Monitor Your Battery Health, Optimize Your Power Usage.

uBattery is a real-time battery monitoring tool designed to offer detailed insights into your device's battery performance. With its dynamic graph and comprehensive data points, it provides users with a deep understanding of battery health and usage, ensuring that you can take proactive measures to preserve battery life and optimize performance.

Key Features of uBattery

  1. Dynamic Battery Monitoring
    • uBattery features a real-time graph that displays the battery percentage from 0% to 100%, allowing users to track their battery's status over time.
    • Users can view the battery's charge rate, discharge rate, voltage, and capacity in real-time, offering a clear picture of how the battery is performing.
  2. Battery Information uBattery provides detailed battery data, including:
    • Charge Rate: Measures the rate at which the battery is receiving electrical energy, typically in milliwatts (mW).
    • Discharge Rate: Indicates how quickly the battery releases energy to power the device, also measured in mW.
    • Voltage: Displays the standard voltage the battery is designed for under optimal conditions.
    • Chemistry: Information on the battery's chemical composition, which affects capacity, voltage, and lifespan.
    • Temperature: Monitors the battery’s temperature, displayed in Celsius.
    • Capacity: Shows the battery’s maximum capacity, indicating how much energy it can store at full charge.
    • Manufacturer and Serial Number: Provides the manufacturer’s name and the unique serial number for each battery unit, ensuring users can track their battery’s origin and identity.
  3. Usage Estimation
    • Estimated Usage: This feature calculates how long the device will last on the remaining battery power based on current usage levels and remaining capacity. It accounts for whether the battery is in a charging or discharging state.
  4. Condition and Alerts
    • Battery Condition: Displays the health of the battery, whether it is operating normally or in need of attention.
    • Alert System: Includes early warning signs with "L" for low battery and "W" for critical battery conditions, allowing users to take timely action before the battery reaches critical levels.
  5. Export and Save Reports
    • uBattery allows users to export battery data in binary format, making it easy to store records or share with other tools for further analysis.

Additional Information

  • Full Charge Capacity: uBattery tracks the Full Charge Capacity, measured in milliampere-hours (mAh) or watt-hours (Wh), to indicate the maximum energy the battery can store when fully charged.
  • Power Source Monitoring: The tool can also show whether the battery is connected to A/C Power, giving users insights into whether the device is running on battery or external power.
  • Remaining Time Tracking: The app tracks the movement of the battery indicator from the start of the monitoring process, providing additional insight into battery usage patterns over time

Why Use uBattery?

uBattery is an invaluable tool for anyone looking to closely monitor their device’s battery health. It provides real-time data, offering insights into charge and discharge rates, voltage, temperature, and overall capacity. Additionally, the tool's ability to export data and provide early warnings for low or critical battery levels ensures that users can take action before their battery performance degrades.

With its simple yet effective interface, uBattery is perfect for anyone who wants to stay informed about their device’s battery status, helping to maintain longer battery life and avoid unexpected shutdowns.
