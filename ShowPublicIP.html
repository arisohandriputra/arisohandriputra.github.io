Tech-savvy users are no strangers to using the command prompt in Windows to gather network details. However, even the most experienced users sometimes prefer a more user-friendly interface over the black, text-based screen of the cmd. That's where ShowPublic IP comes in.

What makes ShowPublic IP special?

  1. Easy-to-Use GUI
    ShowPublic IP is designed to provide all the important network information you need with just a few clicks. The user-friendly interface makes it simple for anyone to find their public IP address and location without needing to navigate the command prompt.
  2. Instant Access to Public IP Information
    Once installed, you can place ShowPublic IP on your desktop for quick access. With just a click, the app will display your public IP address along with useful geographical information, such as:
    • City
    • Region
    • Country
    • Geographical coordinates
    • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
      Additionally, the time zone of your location is also shown.
  3. Copy and Share Information with Ease
    Every piece of information displayed has a "copy" button next to it, allowing you to quickly send the details to your Windows clipboard. This makes it easy to use or share the information anywhere you need it.
  4. Test Your VPN Connection
    If you're using a VPN to hide your real IP address and location, ShowPublic IP can help you verify if the VPN is working properly. Simply click the "Refresh" button to reload the details, checking if the IP address and location have changed to reflect your VPN connection.

Who is it for?

  • General users: For those who want to quickly find their public IP address without using the command prompt.
  • VPN users: If you're using a VPN and need to verify if it's functioning correctly, this app helps check if your public IP and location match the VPN server.

  1. Launch the App
    Simply open ShowPublic IP from your desktop.
  2. View Your IP and Location
    The app will immediately show your public IP address along with your city, region, country, and other details.
