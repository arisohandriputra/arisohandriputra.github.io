While streaming services dominate the way we consume music today, there’s still value in maintaining a local music library. Whether it’s to ensure uninterrupted access to your favorite tracks or to preserve songs that might disappear from streaming platforms, having your collection locally stored brings peace of mind.

Organize Your Library with Ease

SoundTag is a user-friendly application designed to help you organize and tag your audio files efficiently. With its straightforward interface, SoundTag makes it easy to manage your music metadata, ensuring your library stays neat and accessible.

Key Features of SoundTag

  1. Customizable Tags
    SoundTag enables you to edit essential metadata fields like title, artist, album, genre, and release year. You can also upload cover art to enhance your library’s visual appeal. All changes are made directly within the interface for convenience.

  2. Manual Data Entry
    Unlike some tagging apps that rely on online databases, SoundTag focuses on manual input, giving you complete control over the information in your library. While this means more effort, it also ensures precision and personalization.

  3. Add Lyrics to Your Tracks
    Lyrics are an integral part of many users’ music experiences. With SoundTag, you can manually add lyrics to your audio files or use the built-in Genius Lyrics search function. A simple press of F6 will direct you to the Genius Lyrics page for the selected song.

  4. Simplicity and Accessibility
    The minimalist design of SoundTag keeps all tools and features within easy reach, making it an excellent choice for users who prefer a straightforward approach to music tagging.

How to Use SoundTag

  • Select Songs to Tag: Start by adding the songs you want to organize to the application.
  • Edit Metadata: Adjust the file’s metadata fields, including title, album, artist, and more. You can even attach cover art to personalize your library.
  • Add Lyrics: If lyrics are missing, search for them using the Genius Lyrics shortcut or input them manually

SoundTag is a practical solution for users who value simplicity and control over their music metadata. While it lacks automated tagging and database integration, its manual approach ensures that your library is curated exactly the way you want it.

If you’re looking for a lightweight and intuitive tool to organize and personalize your music collection, SoundTag is a reliable choice to keep your library neat and tidy.
