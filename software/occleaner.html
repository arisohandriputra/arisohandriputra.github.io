OneClick Cleaner

Clean junk files and free up space to keep your PC running fast.

Over time, your computer accumulates unnecessary files such as temporary data, browser caches, Windows logs, and error reports. These can slow down your system and consume valuable disk space. OneClick Cleaner is designed to resolve this quickly and effectively—just launch the app and let it do the rest.

As the name suggests, OneClick Cleaner works automatically. Simply double-click the executable and the application will begin scanning your system for junk files and remove them without requiring any further input. There are no settings to configure and no prompts to confirm—the cleaning begins instantly.

During the process, you can view the scanning progress in real time, and once complete, the app will display a summary showing how many junk files were found and deleted.

Whether your PC feels sluggish or you just want to free up some space, OneClick Cleaner provides a fast, no-hassle solution to regain speed and efficiency on your machine.
